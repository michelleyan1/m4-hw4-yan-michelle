About

Life

Born Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini, Doja Cat is an American rapper and singer. She was born in Los Angeles and experienced harsh racism and a mixed-race person, causing her to move away from tradition school into dancing. She eventually dropped out of school at the age of 16 to pursue the arts.

Career

She began making music on SoundCloud when she was a teenager. Signed by Kemosabe and RCA Records at the young age of 17, she started recording music and earned success with her 2018 single "Mooo!" which went viral on the internet. After her success, she quickly release Hot Pink (2018) a year later. She performed with Lizzo on her tour in 2017.

Awards

  • Five Billboard Music Awards
  • Five American Music Awards
